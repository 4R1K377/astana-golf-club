About

About Us

Discover the club's story, our values, and the people behind the experience. Here you'll find an introduction to our heritage, facilities, and what makes Astana Golf Club a place to play, meet, and unwind.

Our Story

Astana Golf Club is the best place to play golf in Kazakhstan, and it's located on the edge of the city of Astana. It offers top-notch sports and elegant relaxation. The club was built to international standards and is located just minutes from the capital city in an expansive steppe landscape. It gives golfers an amazing experience. The 18-hole championship course at Astana Golf Club was designed to be difficult for professional players but fun for golfers of all skill levels. In addition to the fairways, the club has a modern clubhouse, fine dining, practice areas, and places for private events. Astana Golf Club was built to raise the level of golf culture in the area. It has become a center for sports, society, and business, and its members and guests experience tradition and innovation in every detail.

Team

The people who work and play there every day shape what Astana Golf Club is like. Our team is made up of a wide range of professionals who truly love golf, hospitality, and making important memories. Each person helps to create the club's mood, whether they are looking after the course, greeting visitors, planning activities, or helping members out of sight. We are all linked by our desire to pay close attention to things, honor the past, and make sure that every visit feels familiar and easy. We build a place where the spirit of the game is matched by warmth, professionalism, and pride in our work.

Careers

The Astana Golf Club makes its members feel valued, trusted, and motivated. We think that a good team culture where everyone's work is valued is the best way to start providing great service. Our club is a place for people who value quality, enjoy their work, and like being part of something elegant but friendly. The Astana Golf Club is a one-of-a-kind place where you can grow your career while having a calm and balanced work life, since it is located in an open landscape.

Growth & Development

We believe in growing together at Astana Golf Club. Members of the team are urged to learn new things, take on duties, and look at other parts of the club. We believe that doing things by hand, internal training, and learning about how golf and hospitality are done around the world are all great ways to learn. Many jobs allow you to grow personally and professionally in a friendly and work-like setting.
