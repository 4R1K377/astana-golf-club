Dining

Dining at Astana Golf Club

At Astana Golf Club, the food and drink options are meant to go along with your day on the course, from small snacks after a round to big meals and drinks with friends. We focus on quality comfort food and a welcoming club atmosphere on our menu. This makes our space great for casual lunches, evening get-togethers, or private parties.

Bar & Lounge

The Bar & Lounge is the heart of everyday life at Astana Golf Club. Open daily, it is the preferred spot for members and guests to relax before or after their round. Enjoy freshly prepared meals, hot drinks, soft drinks, and a curated selection of beverages in a casual, comfortable setting. Whether you're stopping by for a quick bite or spending the evening with friends, the Bar & Lounge offers an easygoing and social atmosphere.

Dining Information

Location: Located near the driving range and clubhouse

Format: Casual dining & bar service

Access: Open to members and guests

Dress Code: Smart Casual attire is required which includes collared shirt, casual slacks or tasteful denim and close toed shoes in good condition. Corresponding attire for women is requested. Women may wear sandals or open-toed shoes, but men are required to wear closed-toe footwear. Shorts, tee shirts, and hats are not permitted.

Usage: Ideal for post-round meals, informal meetings, and small gatherings
