Welcome to

Astana Golf Club

Where the City Comes to Play

Just a short drive from the center of the capital, Astana Golf Club brings together championship golf, a relaxed atmosphere and warm Kazakh hospitality. Wide fairways, carefully prepared greens and modern practice facilities make the club welcoming both for newcomers to the game and for experienced players who train regularly and compete in tournaments. Beyond the course, guests enjoy a clubhouse with panoramic views, a restaurant and terrace, a pro shop and spaces for family days, corporate events and private celebrations – everything you need to spend the whole day in one place.

About the Club

Golf, Dining and Events in One Destination

At Astana Golf Club, an 18-hole course, driving range and short-game areas sit alongside a modern clubhouse, restaurant and pro shop. Players can start the day on the practice range, head out for a relaxed round or friendly competition, and finish with dinner overlooking the course. The club regularly hosts member tournaments, junior programs, business events and family celebrations, creating a community around the game and a welcoming place to spend time together on and off the fairways.
